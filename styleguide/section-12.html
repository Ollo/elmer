12.0 - Wells / Boxes

custom layout containers

12.1 - @mixin well($radius, $padding, $margin, $full_border, $background);

This mixin is used on wells as well as various other elements. $radius is 6px by default $padding uses a common well padding of 15px on mobile sizes/ 25px on medium-large sizes. Use 'true' or 'false' if you want this padding. $margin puts a simple 1rem bottom margin on the well. Use either 'true' or 'false' if you want this spacing. $full_border adds a 1px solid $light_gray border. Use true if you want this border, if false an opaque bottom border is added. $background is $white by default

12.2 - %well_white, %well_white_no_padding

Extend these wells as needed, as seen below.

12.4 - .box

A more complex well style that holds rows but does not span the entire width. Header variations available as seen in _typography.scss.

I am a blue header
I am a row
I am also a row
<div class="box">
	<div class="muted_header primary_variation">I am a blue header</div>
	<div class="muted_header secondary_variation"><input type="text" placeholder="Search..."/></div>
	<div class="row">I am a row</div>
	<div class="row">I am also a row</div>
</div>
