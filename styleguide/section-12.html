12.0 - Wells / Boxes

custom layout containers

12.1 - .well

Base well class. Mix and match various well classes for desired affect.

This is a well. (Red border is for display only and not to be used)
<div class="well" style="border: 1px dashed red;">This is a well. (Red border is for display only and not to be used)</div>

12.2 - .well.shadow

White background with a shadow.
<div class="well shadow">White background with a shadow.</div>

12.3 - .well.bottom_border

White background with a bottom border (opacity .2). Border doesn't show here but works propelry on the site.
<div class="well bottom_border">White background with a bottom border (opacity .2). Border doesn't show here but works propelry on the site.</div>

12.4 - .well.gray_outline

White background with full border and no internal padding, used to outline QOTD tables.
<div class="well gray_outline">White background with full border and no internal padding, used to outline QOTD tables.</div>

12.5 - .well.inset (not in use)

White background with an inset shadow.
<div class="well inset">White background with an inset shadow.</div>

12.6 - .well.green

White background with a green border.
<div class="well green">White background with a green border.</div>

12.7 - .well.blue

Blue background with a gray border.
<div class="well blue">Blue background with a gray border.</div>

12.8 - .box

A more complex well style that holds rows but does not span the entire width. Header variations available as seen in _typography.scss.

I am a blue header
I am a row
I am also a row 
<div class="box">
	    <div class="muted_header primary_variation">I am a blue header</div>
     <div class="muted_header secondary_variation"><input type="text" placeholder="Search..."/></div>
     <div class="row">
         I am a row
     </div>
     <div class="row">
         I am also a row
     </div>
</div>
